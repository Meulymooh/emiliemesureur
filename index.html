Education

Training in Web Development

BeCode, Ghent (Belgium) - 2019

BeCode helps highly motivated and independent people become junior web developers within 6 months. It is based on the Simplon method stressing the benefit of "learning to learn" so as to avoid being stuck in the future when confronted with other digital languages. The program first covers the basics of front-end and back-end development before the students choose their own specialization.

Master in Translation and International Relations

Formerly an independent school, ISTI is now the Translation and Intepretation department of the Free University of Brussels. During my master, I worked on two main projects: a research and translation thesis about corruption in the US institutions, and a research work about the importance of the French language within the organization "Canadian Parents for French".

Bachelor in English and American studies

I majored in American literature and history. The curriculum covered the following subjects: grammar, phonetics, British and American literature, British and American history, translation.
